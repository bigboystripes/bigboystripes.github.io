The Sunday Love Club is a community-driven organization based in State College, dedicated to fostering creativity, collaboration, and connection. Our mission is to bring people together through art, music, and shared experiences. The sunday Love club

The sunday Love club has 4 founding members, Youngjin, Jacob, Cyrus, and Sergio. Despite being born in different towns, and being different ages, they all met in State College, PA. They all share a love for music, and a passion for creating art.
